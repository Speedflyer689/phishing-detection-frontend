Understanding Phishing Threats

Phishing is a cybercrime where attackers masquerade as legitimate institutions to steal sensitive information such as login credentials, credit card details, and personal data. These attacks can lead to:

  • Identity theft and financial fraud
  • Unauthorized access to personal accounts
  • Installation of malware on your devices
  • Corporate data breaches

Know the Difference

Phishing

Targeted attacks that impersonate legitimate organizations to steal sensitive information. Often includes:

  • Fake login pages
  • Urgent action requests
  • Professional appearance

Scam

Fraudulent schemes designed to trick people into sending money or personal information. Usually involves:

  • False promises
  • Get-rich-quick schemes
  • Fake investments

Spam

Unsolicited bulk messages, often for commercial purposes. Characterized by:

  • Mass distribution
  • Promotional content
  • Less sophisticated targeting
